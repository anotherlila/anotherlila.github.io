No bake cookies

This is a famous Evans family recipe.No bake cookies became popular during the depresion as it took few ingredents to hold it together. No bake cookies usually consits of oats, peanut butter, and butter. You can learn more about the history Here


You can modify this recipe for diatary ristrctions as needed. For example I am going to modify the recipe to be gluten free for my family member. So I will use gluten free oats, vinnila and peanutbutter. You can use normal ingredents as you wish and it should not impact the taste. The pictures are when me and my sister made a video about making No Bake Cookies 3 years ago. it was made with an ipad so the quality is bad.

ingredients

  • 1 3/4cups of white sugar
  • 1/2 cup of butter
  • 1/2 cup of milk
  • 4 tablespoons of unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1/2 of peanutbutter
  • 3 cups of quick oats
  • 1 teaspoon Vanilla extract
  • Wax paper

Directions

  1. Line 2 cookie sheets with wax paper. Set aside.
  2. In a large bowl combine peanutbutter, oats and vanilla extract. Set aside
  3. in a suace pan mix sugar, butter, milk, and cocoa on ,meduim heat till boiling. Boil for a minute and a half.
  4. Remove from heat and transfer into the large bowl and mix intell well combined.
  5. take spoonfulls and drop them on the wax paper.
  6. Let them cool intill hardened.
